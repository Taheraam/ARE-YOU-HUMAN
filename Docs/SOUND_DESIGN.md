# SOUND DESIGN

## Philosophy
Audio should evolve with chaos.

The soundscape should begin subtle and become psychologically overwhelming.

---

## Early Sounds
- soft clicks
- clean notifications
- UI hover sounds
- minimal ambience

---

## Mid Sounds
- distorted static
- delayed button audio
- subtle machine hum
- glitch transitions

---

## Final Sounds
- corrupted startup tones
- warning alarms
- reversed audio
- unstable ambience
- distorted breathing textures

---

## Important Rule
Never make audio too loud or annoying.

The goal is discomfort, not irritation.
