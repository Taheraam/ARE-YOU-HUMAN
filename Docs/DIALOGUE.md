# DIALOGUE STYLE

## Tone
The system behaves like:
- corporate software
- emotionally detached AI
- suspicious observer
- unstable verification system

Avoid:
- cringe memes
- internet slang
- random jokes
- overexplaining humor

---

## Dialogue Examples

### Early Stage
"Verification required."
"Please confirm your humanity."
"Behavior analysis in progress."

### Mid Stage
"Your hesitation appears organic."
"Confidence level unusually synthetic."
"Most humans solve this faster."

### Late Stage
"Why are you still trying?"
"We are beginning to doubt ourselves."
"Verification has become emotionally difficult."

### Final Stage
"You continued despite the instability."
"Human persistence detected."
"Perhaps neither of us passed."
